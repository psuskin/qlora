loggisto: Warehouse movements are storage, removal, stock transfers, inventories and warehouse execution documents, whereby the latter are only used as part of the Hestia WMS. Each stock movement is posted directly to the part, to the SLM (if available) and to the storage bin as an activity. List window: This window is used to search for and list stock movements and can be called up from a part/PC (movements of a part/PC) or a warehouse (movements in a warehouse across parts and storage bins in the warehouse) or a storage bin (movements in a storage bin across all parts). Module name: stockTransactionList.app. Classes: CX_TRANSACTION.
