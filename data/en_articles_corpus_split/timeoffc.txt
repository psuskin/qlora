timeoffc: This app compares the planned and absent times of employees within the ClassiX Personnel Time Management and - if the option is set accordingly - the payroll period is stored as the last completed payroll period for the respective employee. Individual absence accounting: With this app, an individual employee is accounted for with regard to his or her absences. Absenteeism accounting: With this app, previously selected employees are accounted for in relation to their absence times. Module name: timeoffc.mod. Classes: CX_EMPLOYEE.
