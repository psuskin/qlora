serviitt: This module is used to maintain service call items. Service call items are created using service provider groups which are assigned to a service call. In this way, services to be performed internally such as installation and commissioning can be scheduled. Input window: The editing window of the service call items is similar in structure and scope of functions to that of the offer and order items. Module name: serviiedt.mod. Classes: CX_LINK_TXN.
