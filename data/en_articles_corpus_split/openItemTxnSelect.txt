openItemTxnSelect: This app can be used to search specifically for individual open items. This app is called from other apps and is used to search for both debit-side and credit-side open items. Selection window: This window is used to select open items. Whether the search is for a customer or vendor open item can be seen from the specification in the window title (e.g. "Select open item documents vendors"). Module name: openItemTxnSelect.app. Classes: CX_OPEN_ITEM_TXN.
