telecomEdit: Input window for telecommunication objects. Telecommunications objects can be objects of the type telephone, fax, mobile phone, e-mail address, www Internet address, etc. Telecommunications objects are assigned to other business objects, whereby a telecommunications object can be assigned as often as desired (see list window Owner). Input window for telecommunication data: This first window is used to maintain the connection data of telecommunication objects. In the second window tab, you can assign additional owners to the telecommunications object. List window owner: In this window, the list of business objects assigned to a telecommunication object can be maintained. At least one business object is always assigned to a telecommunication object. Further business objects can be inserted via the "Insert" button, the context menu in the list or via drag&drop. Business objects of the type company, person and industrial plant are permitted. Module name: telecomEdit.app. Classes: CX_TELECOM.
