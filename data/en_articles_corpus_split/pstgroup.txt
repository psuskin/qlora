pstgroup: The purchasing statistics groups take over the functions of the initial account assignment to cost elements/g/L accounts. This is made possible by the fact that they are assigned to the purchasing roles or their parts by means of the classification term "parts classification" (ITEM_CLASSIFICATION). Input window: This window is used to maintain purchasing statistics groups. List window: This window is used to list purchasing statistics groups. Selection window: This window is used to select purchasing statistics groups. Module name: pstgroup.mod. Classes: -.
