Warranty: The warranty or liability for defects determines legal consequences and claims to which the buyer is entitled within the framework of a sales contract in which the seller has delivered defective goods or items. In the case of a contract for work and services, there is also a warranty for defects in the manufactured work. The warranty is to be distinguished from the legally prescribed warranty as a voluntary service. Specific fields Example: 12 months after acceptance or 18 months after delivery, 1-layer, with the exception of wear parts. Acceptance date must be posted in writing.
