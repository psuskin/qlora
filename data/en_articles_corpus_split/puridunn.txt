puridunn: Suppliers can be reminded if they have either not responded to the requested price request or have not submitted the requested supplier declaration. Input window: Please select the request items to be reminded. Afterwards please press the print button. A reminder will then be printed and the dunning level will be automatically increased. Position window: Here you can enter an additional specific dunning text for each item, change the dunning level manually or, for example, set or revoke a dunning block. Module name: puridunn.mod. Classes: CX_PURCHASE_INQUIRY.
