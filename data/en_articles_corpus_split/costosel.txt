costosel: Within the CyberEnterprise business OS there are 4 types of different cost objects: sales cost objects (projects), purchase cost objects (contracts), internal cost objects and marketing cost objects (sales opportunities). These different types of cost objects are managed in their own modules, with this module cost objects of different types can be listed or selected together. List-/Selection window: This window is used to list or select the different types of cost units. Option window: All settings for the search can be made here. Module name: costObjectiveSelect.app. Classes: CX_SALES_COST_OBJECTIVE, CX_PURCHASE_COST_OBJECTIVE, CX_INTERNAL_COST_OBJECTIVE or CX_MARKETING_COST_OBJECTIVE.
