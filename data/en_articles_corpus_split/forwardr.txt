forwardr. Input window: This module is used to maintain the carrier data. You can select a forwarding agent by entering the forwarding agent number in the Number field and confirming with RETURN. Fields marked with "italics" represent data directly from the company. If something is changed in one of these fields, the change is applied to all roles and the company itself.
