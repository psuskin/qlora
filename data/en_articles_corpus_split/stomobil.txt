stomobil: This window allows to process and post a storage, retrieval, stock transfer document as well as an inventory document (counting list) by means of a mobile barcode scanner and the printout of the document. Input window: In the window heading you can see which document we are currently processing, e.g. "Storage", "Removal from storage" etc. Module name: stomobil.mod. Security: In addition to restricting access rights via the class and its data fields, the module can be restricted in its use via some of the received messages.
