cxCreditCardAccount: This module is used to maintain credit cards of partner objects (companies or persons). Credit card objects belong to the class of communication objects, such as address, telephone or e-mail address.The information to be entered includes the type of credit card, card holder, card number and expiry date.Functionality Input window List window. Input window: This window is used for maintaining bank details. List window: This window is used to search for the holders of a credit card. Module name: cardaedt.mod. Classes: CX_CREDIT_CARD_ACCOUNT.
