busiseg: The various sales areas or markets can be defined via the business segments. The focus should be on the own definition of the markets without having to take into account generally valid definitions such as branches. Each turnover is assigned to a business segment, this is done by means of the definition in the client, customer or cost unit. A tree structure can be created through the hierarchical linking of the business areas. The following table shows you when which values are booked in the business segments or in the statistics: Document Status Offers Bid submitted Offer converted into order Orders Order confirmed Invoices Invoice registered. Input window: This window is used to maintain business areas. You can select a business area by entering the desired number in the "Number" field and confirming with RETURN. If you want to call up the list window, you can select the menu item "Search". List window: Used to list the business fields. Selection window: This window is for selecting a cost center.
