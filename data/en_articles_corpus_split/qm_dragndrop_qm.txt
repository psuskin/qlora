qm_dragndrop_qm: With this you can test drag & drop between various widgets. After calling it up, you can create a sample data structure via the 'File' menu. With the right mouse button and 'Show structure' these example data can be filled into the individual window objects. Now you can drag single elements of this data in and between the windows. With the button at the bottom right you can control the handling of Drag & Drop with Throws. Module name: reload_qm.mod.
