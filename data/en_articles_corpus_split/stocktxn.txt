stocktxn: With this module, all types of stock movements still to be processed (stock placements, stock removals and stock transfers) can be listed at a glance. In particular, this module is used within the framework of the creation of transfer orders. List window: This window is used to list the stock movements still to be processed. Option window: All settings for the selection of stock movements can be made here. Module name: stocktxn.mod. Classes: CX_STOCK_INPUT.
