cobjrept: For individual cost units - separated by cost elements - the accumulated costs posted in financial accounting are displayed. The financial accounting costs can be delimited according to posting periods "from-to" or in total up to one posting period. The cost elements can be output either as a list of one another or structured by means of a classification term (e.g. P&L, BWA, etc.). Module name: cobjrept.mod. Classes: CX_TRANSACTION.
