geleddep: This module is used to maintain the G/L accounts defined as cash accounts in financial accounting. The special feature of these cash accounts is that they can be directly assigned to your own business accounts at banks or a cash book. Such assignments are suitable, for example, for automated payment transactions within the framework of online banking. If money accounts in foreign currency are posted to, the respective foreign currency amount of a posting is also displayed in the list view. Input window: This window is used to maintain the money G/L accounts in financial accounting. Module name: geleddep.mod. Classes: CX_GENERAL_LEDGER.
