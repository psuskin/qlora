databaseManage: With the help of the database management, the current database can be backed up at any time and reset to a backup. A backup can be performed during operation. When resetting, all applications accessing the database must be terminated. In the classix.cloud, access for other users is automatically blocked during recovery and released again after recovery. Input window: This window is used for archiving databases and maintaining the database archive. Module name: databaseManage.app.
