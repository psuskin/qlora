assetAccountTxnList: Postings within financial accounting to accounts for fixed assets must be reconciled with the data in fixed asset accounting. To facilitate this work, this module lists the postings to a freely selectable balance sheet asset account with a direct assignment to the respective open item. Only general ledger entries, incoming invoices, credit notes and payments from suppliers are shown. List window: This window is used to evaluate assets. Module name: assetrep.mod.
