purchinvitem: Incoming invoice items can be entered manually or transferred from purchase orders. Input window: This window is for maintaining purchase invoice items. You can create a new A/P invoice item by filling in the required fields completely or by selecting a purchase order. Fast entry: This window is used to quickly enter free positions. Replace predecessor: This window is used to exchange predecessors of purchase invoice items that have already been completed. Distribution of cost units ("normal" incoming invoice items): Currently, "normal" incoming invoice items are recorded in a 1:1 relationship with the delivery schedule lines of the order. However, since these can have several cost object items (order items/stock order items) as predecessors, the proportion of cost objects in the incoming invoice item must be determined when the incoming invoice is entered.
