corporateGroupEdit: This app can be used to maintain the relationships of affiliated companies. For this purpose, it is possible to specify for companies which other company is the parent or parent company or which other companies are subordinates or subsidiaries. This information is used, for example, in the account assignment of outgoing or incoming invoices. The term 'associated enterprises' should not be confused with the term 'branch'. Enterprises are affiliated if they are independent companies that cooperate with each other through participations. Branches are locations/departments of one and the same company. Branches are maintained in the company master module. Input window: This window is used to maintain the relationships of affiliated companies. Module name: corpgrup.mod. Classes: CX_CORPORATE_GROUP.
