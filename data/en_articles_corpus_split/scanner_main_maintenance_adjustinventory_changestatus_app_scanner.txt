scanner_main_maintenance_adjustinventory_changestatus_app_scanner: To change the condition of an article or a partial quantity of an article in a storage bin (e.g. if a part becomes defective or is to be blocked etc.) the storage bin and article are scanned. The quantity with assigned status is displayed. By selecting the appropriate line, the next screen appears, which allows to select the new status in one line. At the end, the mask appears that allows to enter the quantity of the item that should change state.Changes of state are booked directly in the host system with the quantities entered.
