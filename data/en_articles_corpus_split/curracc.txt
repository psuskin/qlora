curracc: Current or cash accounts are managed by banks and the "custodian" partner role assigned to them. Their main function in ClassiX® is to maintain the connection to the money accounts in the financial accounting. Bank statements provided electronically by banks are assigned to the accounts maintained here after being read into ClassiX®(bank statements) and can also be viewed here. Input window: This window is used to maintain current accounts. It is divided into the three partial flaps. Excerpts: If bank statements have been imported for the selected account, they are displayed in this window separately by posting year. List window: Serves to list the current accounts. Module name: curracc.mod. Classes: CX_CURRENT_ACCOUNT.
