cxCampaign: In a campaign you can write to different people with one form. In contrast to the transaction document, the contents of the individual mails are saved for each person. This is why this campaign is preferable to the normal transaction document if the contents per person differ greatly and are snapshots of a certain data situation. Functionality Input window List window Selection window Option window. Input window: Here the data for the campaign can be recorded and managed. List window: Listing of the campaigns to display them with a double click or with the "OK" button. Selection window: Listing of the campaigns to select them with a double click or with the "OK" button.
