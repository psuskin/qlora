This is the description of the module "depositAccountEdit" with the name "Cash account": This module is used to maintain the G/L accounts defined as cash accounts in financial accounting. The special feature of these cash accounts is that they can be assigned directly to your own business accounts at banks or a cash book. Such allocations are suitable, for example, for automated payment transactions in the context of online banking.If cash accounts are posted to in foreign currency, the respective foreign currency amount of a posting is also displayed in the list view.
