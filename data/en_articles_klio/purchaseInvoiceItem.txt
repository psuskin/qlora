This is the description of the module "purchaseInvoiceItem" with the name "Receipt invoice and receipt credit items": Incoming invoice items can be entered manually or transferred from purchase orders. This is the description of the functionality of the module "purchaseInvoiceItem" with the name "Receipt invoice and receipt credit items" regarding Input window: This window is for maintaining purchase invoice items. You can create a new A/P invoice item by filling in the required fields completely or by selecting a purchase order. This is the description of the functionality of the module "purchaseInvoiceItem" with the name "Receipt invoice and receipt credit items" regarding Fast entry: This window is used to quickly enter free positions. This is the description of the functionality of the module "purchaseInvoiceItem" with the name "Receipt invoice and receipt credit items" regarding Replace predecessor: This window is used to exchange predecessors of purchase invoice items that have already been completed. This is the description of the functionality of the module "purchaseInvoiceItem" with the name "Receipt invoice and receipt credit items" regarding Distribution of cost units ("normal" incoming invoice items): Currently, "normal" incoming invoice items are recorded in a 1:1 relationship with the delivery schedule lines of the order. However, since these can have several cost object items (order items/stock order items) as predecessors, the proportion of cost objects in the incoming invoice item must be determined when the incoming invoice is entered.
