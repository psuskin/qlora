This is the description of the module "parquery" with the name "Partner research": Through the partner search, ClassiX® offers its users the possibility to research contact persons and companies via complex query options. First and foremost, ClassiX® offers the possibility to search for partners via their respective search masks. Thus, there are search masks to search directly for companies and persons as well as the respective roles (contact person, interested parties, customers, etc.). Via the search mask, these search options can now be combined as desired.
