This is the description of the functionality of the module "scanner_main_maintenance_relocate_looseitems_app_scanner" with the name "Move scanner parts" regarding Transferring individual parts: The stock to be transferred remains posted in the source bin until the destination bin is scanned. If there are already reservations for orders to be picked in the source storage bin, stock can still be transferred. As soon as picking is to be carried out from this bin and the quantities are insufficient, the picker is sent to the new storage bin.
