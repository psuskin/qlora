This is the description of the module "cxCommissionTxn" with the name "Commission agreement": The commission agreement is the document in ClassiXÂ® that records the client's agreement with an agent to pay commission. As a rule, the commission agreement is the successor to an order, which is therefore the basis of the agreement. However, an order can also be inserted as a predecessor, i.e. if a commission recipient is to receive a commission for the processing of a procurement transaction.The amount of the commission is defined via the commission agreement. Settlement takes place via the creation of one (or more) outgoing credit notes.Commission agreements can be created manually or automatically. They are created automatically if one or more representatives are assigned to a customer. When an order is created for this customer, a commission agreement is automatically created for each of these representatives. The amount of the commission is determined by the standard commission of the representative. However, the amount of the commission can also be specified individually for individual customers.Commission agreements are also created automatically when one or more agents are assigned to an order.
