This is the description of the class "Types Diffuser Date periods": A date period always has the type "DAY" because it always describes "one day". In contrast, a date period can have a period length of a different type if it is diffuse. All possible types and the values assigned to them are listed in the following table. Type Value The date consists of a sequence of DAY 19 Meeting WEEK 20 weeks MONTH 23 months QUARTER 24 Quarters TERTIAN 25 Tertials YEAR 26 years DECADE 27 Decades CENTURY 28 Centuries MILLENIUM 29 millennia.
