This is the description of the functionality of the class "CX_SITE - Space" regarding Description: Place means the verbal description of a location that cannot be or does not need to be described by a street address, post office box address or geographical location. CX_SITE makes groupings: e.g. branches of a company, different access routes in the case of large company premises, telephone exchanges). If a CX_CORPORATION object has different CX_SITE objects, these can be assigned by slot (and functions) from CX_CUSTOMER or CX_SUPPLIER as invoice, payment or dunning recipients. Employees (CX_PERSON) of a partner company are employees of a CX_CORPORATION object. The CX_CORPORATION object can have different CX_SITE objects (branches). The CX_PERSON objects can also be assigned the corresponding CX_SITE (possibly with CX_WRAPPER as the workplace, another address as the private address) via access.
