This is the description of the functionality of the class "CX_WORK_TIME_KEEPER*" regarding Description: (Business Pattern): This class represents working time keepers. It is defined in addition to the role of an employee (CX_STAFF_MEMBER) in order to be able to assign, among other things, the corresponding working time payroll rules(CX_WORK_TIME_RULE) and the working time model(CX_WORK_TIME_MODEL) to an employee.
