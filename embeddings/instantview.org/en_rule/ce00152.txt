This is the description of the class "CX_SPAN_DATE - Day type specifications": The functions summarised in this section provide information on whether the date is a special holiday and what type of date it is. Function name Description IsDateType Is the date a "special day off"? IsTerm Is the date an appointment? IsAnniversary Is the date an anniversary? IsHoliday Is the date a public holiday? IsVacation Is the date a holiday? IsWorkingday Is the date a working day? TypeOfDate Type of date.
