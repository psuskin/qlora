This is the description of the functionality of the class "CX_TELECOM - Telecommunications" regarding Description: CX_TELECOM describes the possibility to contact a partner electronically. There are the following types of telecommunication: The date, be it a telephone number, email address or similar, is stored in the fixed data field "uniqueID". However, the programmer should avoid accessing this data field directly. A number of functions are available for read and write access, all of which have the abbreviation "ID" in them. Normally, the MA function FormattedIDMA should suffice, other special functions are listed in the MDI. Some subclasses have their own derivations with more differentiated behaviour and sometimes have further functions.
