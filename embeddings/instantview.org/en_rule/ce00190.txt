This is the description of the class "CX_PERIODIC_DATE - Information": The functions summarised in this section provide information about a periodic date. Function name Description DayConsistsOfWeekdays Does the date include a day of the week? DayIsASpecialDay Does the date include a day? IsCompact Is the date "compact"? IsUndef Is the date undefined?
