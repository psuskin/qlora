This is the description of the class "Arithmetic operations": In principle, any objects can be linked by the mathematical operators "+", "-", "*" and "/". In certain cases, however, certain operations are excluded. For example, it does not make sense to multiply two dates, but the difference between them can be calculated as the number of days and is therefore reasonable. Objects that belong to one of the following areas can be linked to other objects using mathematical operators. Dates Time data Amounts.
