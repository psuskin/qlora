This is the description of the functionality of the class "CX_DICTIONARY_CI" regarding Description: The class CX_DICTIONARY_CI corresponds to the class CX_DICTIONARY, with the only difference that when searching in the dictionary, no distinction is made between upper and lower case letters (case insensitive). However, the dictionary retains the upper/lower case of the terms as they are entered. CX_DICTIONARY_CI belongs to the family of dictionary classes.
